FAQ

Common Questions

What types of projects do you handle?

I focus on end-to-end data architecture and analysis. This includes custom GA4 and GTM (Web & Server-Side) implementations, automated ETL pipelines using Power Query/Python, SQL-based attribution modeling, and dynamic Power BI executive dashboards.

Do you work with messy exported files?

Yes, but my goal is to eliminate them. I take fragmented data—like inconsistent CSVs, messy CRM exports, and overlapping ad platform reports—and build automated pipelines that clean, standardize, and load that data directly into your reporting tools so you never have to do it manually again.

Can you audit an existing GA4/GTM setup?

Absolutely. I audit for broken event taxonomy, missing lead-generation triggers, misattribution errors, and data layer gaps. The goal is to ensure your tracking pipeline feeds clean, model-ready data into your warehouse or BI tools.

Do you provide documentation and handoff?

Yes. High-level data architecture is useless if your team can't manage it. I provide comprehensive implementation notes, data dictionaries, QA validation proof, and clear standard operating procedures (SOPs) for every workflow.
